Cookie Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They're commonly used to make sites work, remember preferences, or gather anonymous usage statistics.

2. Cookies on This Site

This website currently does not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. The contact form is submitted directly via Netlify Forms and does not set any cookies as part of that process.

3. If This Changes

If we introduce analytics or other cookie-based tools in future (for example, to understand how visitors use the site), this page will be updated accordingly, and a cookie consent banner will be shown to first-time visitors.

4. Managing Cookies

Should cookies be introduced in future, you will be able to manage or disable them at any time through your browser settings. Instructions vary by browser and are available on each browser provider's support pages.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to info@manstal.co.uk.
