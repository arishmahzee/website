Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

1. Who We Are

Manstal Limited ("we", "us", "our") is a construction and project services company registered in England and Wales. This policy explains what personal data we collect through this website, why, and how we look after it.

2. What We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, we collect:

  • First name and surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number, project type, and indicative budget (business enquiries only)
  • The contents of your message

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics on this site at this time.

3. How We Use It

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your enquiry, discuss your project, and provide a quote or further information where relevant. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. How It's Processed

Form submissions are processed by Netlify Forms and delivered to our team by email. Netlify acts as a data processor on our behalf. You can read Netlify's own privacy policy on their website for details of how they handle submitted data in transit.

5. How Long We Keep It

We retain enquiry data for as long as necessary to respond to you and, where a project proceeds, for the duration of our business relationship plus a reasonable period afterwards for legal and accounting purposes. You can request deletion at any time — see Section 7.

6. Legal Basis

We process your data on the basis of legitimate interest (responding to enquiries you have voluntarily submitted) under UK GDPR.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to how your data is processed

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@manstal.co.uk.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to info@manstal.co.uk or 01234 567890.
